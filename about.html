关于会议 About

我们的会议是人工智能领域综合性的学术盛会，自2019年以来每年举办，汇聚了来自全球各地的顶尖学者与产业领军人物。大会不仅是分享研究成果的平台，也是推动学术交流与合作的重要舞台。 Our conference is a comprehensive academic event in the field of artificial intelligence, held annually since 2019. It brings together leading scholars and industry leaders from around the world. The conference is not only a platform for sharing research results but also an important stage for promoting academic exchange and collaboration.

大会包括主题演讲、专题论坛、工作坊以及展览展示等多种形式，涵盖机器学习、计算机视觉、自然语言处理、具身智能等前沿领域，为参会者提供丰富多样的内容体验。 The conference includes keynote speeches, themed forums, workshops and exhibitions, covering cutting‑edge areas such as machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing and embodied intelligence, providing participants with a rich and diverse experience.
